Anti-Money Laundering (AML) measures for Lucidia crypto project:

Customer Due Diligence (CDD): Lucidia implements a thorough CDD process to verify the identity of clients and assess their financial background to ensure they are not involved in illegal activities.

KYC (Know Your Customer): Lucidia follows strict KYC procedures to collect information about clients and verify their identity. This helps to assess their potential risk as customers and ensure compliance with AML regulations.

Transaction Monitoring: Lucidia closely monitors all transactions to detect and report any suspicious activities. The platform uses advanced algorithms and analytics to flag any unusual or high-risk transactions.

Risk Assessment: Lucidia regularly evaluates the risks associated with clients, businesses, and geographies to determine the level of AML controls required. The platform has a robust risk assessment framework in place to identify and mitigate potential AML risks.

Reporting: Lucidia is committed to complying with all regulatory requirements and reporting any suspicious activities to the relevant authorities. The platform has robust reporting mechanisms in place to ensure prompt and accurate reporting of any suspicious activities.
