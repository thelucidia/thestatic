The main currency in the Lucidia metaverse is the $LUCID token, of which there are a total of 1,000,000,000 (1 billion). At the TGE, tokens will be split between presale, public IDOs, and liquidity. This will make it possible for $LUCID to be traded on DEX and CEXs.<\/span><\/p>

\u00a0<\/p>

Here’s how the last $LUCID tokens will be given out:<\/span><\/p>

\u00a0<\/p>

Public IDOs: 4%; PreSale: 13%; Reserve and Burn: 13%; Liquidity: DEX\/CEX: 5% Development: 10% Team: 6% Marketing and Community: 14% Advisors: 4% Rewards and Staking: 5% System: 9% Operation: 12% Treasury: 10%<\/span><\/p>

This allocation has a 12-month cliff because 13% is set aside as a reserve or to be burned if needed to keep the $LUCID value stable. Also, 9% of $LUCID tokens are given to Lucidia users as in-game rewards and rewards for helping the platform grow, such as staking tokens. In contrast, only 4% of $LUCID tokens are available to the public through IDOs. This shows that Lucidia gives users and the community the most importance.<\/span><\/p>

\u00a0<\/p>

The $LUCID token is still being fine-tuned in terms of how it works. The Lucidia team cares as much about cutting-edge technology and entertainment as they do about system stability, and they know how important tokenomics are to the Lucidia metaverse as a whole. So, they won’t say that the tokenomics are done until they are sure that they meet all of the users’ needs.<\/span><\/p>

\u00a0<\/p>

The money earned by spending $LUCID in-game will be split in the following ways:<\/span><\/p>

\u00a0<\/p>

Buy back and burn to help with liquidity: 40%<\/span><\/p>

Marketing: 25%<\/span><\/p>

Prize pool and system of rewards: 25%<\/span><\/p>

Infrastructure: 10%<\/span><\/p>

Referral Program: 5%<\/span><\/p>

This distribution shows that Lucidia is committed to putting users and the community first.
