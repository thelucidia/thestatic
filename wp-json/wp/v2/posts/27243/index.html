Lucidia Metaverse, which is in the running for the best crypto presale of 2023, uses the freemium model as its main way to get more users and a more diverse group of users. Taking its cue from Facebook’s famous slogan, “It’s free and will always be,” Lucidia wants to change the way people use the internet by giving users a taste of its product.<\/span><\/p>

\u00a0<\/p>

How Freemium Works:<\/span><\/p>

\u00a0<\/p>

The freemium model has several benefits for organic growth and getting more users:<\/span><\/p>

\u00a0<\/p>

Accessible Entry Point: By giving users free access to the metaverse, Lucidia removes barriers to joining the platform and encourages more people to do so.<\/span><\/p>

\u00a0<\/p>

Diverse User Base: A freemium model brings in a wider range of users, making the metaverse community more diverse and full of life.<\/span><\/p>

\u00a0<\/p>

Organic Growth: As more people join and explore the Lucidia metaverse, the platform’s services and experiences will naturally grow, which will bring in more users and keep them interested.<\/span><\/p>

\u00a0<\/p>

Effective Onboarding: Lucidia makes sure that users can quickly and easily dive into the metaverse and start exploring its potential by putting a high priority on a smooth onboarding process.<\/span><\/p>

\u00a0<\/p>

Taking Advantage of the Freemium Trend:<\/span><\/p>

\u00a0<\/p>

Forbes says that freemium is now the most popular way to price software, which makes it a good choice for Lucidia as it tries to change the way the internet works. By giving users a free taste of the metaverse, Lucidia hopes to make them want to try more immersive and interesting things on its platform.<\/span><\/p>

\u00a0<\/p>
