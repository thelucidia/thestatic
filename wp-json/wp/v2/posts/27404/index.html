Lucidia’s mission is to change the face of esports by giving players, teams, and fans an unparalleled virtual experience. Lucidia provides esports fans with a one-of-a-kind and exciting user experience by combining virtual reality, augmented reality, and blockchain technology.<\/span><\/p>

\u00a0<\/p>

Notable Characteristics:<\/span><\/p>

\u00a0<\/p>

Tokens and awards may be tracked and distributed in a transparent manner thanks to Blockchain technology, which also guarantees the ownership and scarcity of digital assets, ensures the safety of financial transactions, and protects the privacy of player information.<\/span><\/p>

\u00a0<\/p>

Earning and Redeeming Tokens Users can earn tokens and benefits by taking part in platform activities including tournaments, playing games, and even hosting their own events.<\/span><\/p>

\u00a0<\/p>

Partnerships: Lucidia has partnered with Playhera and G2 Esports to provide gamers with an unparalleled, individualized gaming experience.<\/span><\/p>

\u00a0<\/p>

Players of EA Sports games may now collaborate with Playhera, an esports hub platform, to host and attend their own virtual events in the EA Sports universe for the chance to win tokens and other benefits.<\/span><\/p>

\u00a0<\/p>

Partnership with G2 Esports: G2 Esports adds its experience running esports competitions to the metaverse, giving users the chance to compete in and watch live esports events, all while earning tokens and incentives for their time and effort.<\/span><\/p>

\u00a0<\/p>
