As the Lucidia community continues to grow and thrive, we’re always looking for ways to bring in more players and make our connections with them stronger. With this in mind, we’re excited to announce the Lucidia Referral Program, which is meant to encourage and reward our loyal players for their dedication to the game and for telling other people about how much they love Lucidia.<\/span><\/p>

\u00a0<\/p>

Participants in the Lucidia Referral Program have a unique chance to earn 5% of all the $LUCID their referrals spend in the game over the course of their lifetime. This not only encourages players to tell their friends about Lucidia, but also gives them a way to make money without doing anything.<\/span><\/p>

\u00a0<\/p>

Here’s how the referral program works:<\/span><\/p>

\u00a0<\/p>

Players who are already part of the Lucidia community will get a unique link they can share with friends, family, and others who want to join.<\/span><\/p>

If a new player signs up using the referral link, the existing player will be credited with the referral.<\/span><\/p>

The player who brings in new players will get 5% of all the $LUCID the new players spend in the game over their lifetime.<\/span><\/p>

The goal of this referral program is to create a system where players are rewarded for being loyal and dedicated to the game, and Lucidia’s user base grows. It gives people who are really into the game a good reason to tell their friends about it, which makes the community stronger and more diverse.<\/span><\/p>

\u00a0<\/p>

We think that this program not only helps people grow, but also makes our community stronger. We’re sure that as more people join us through the referral program, the overall game experience will get better and the world of Lucidia will keep changing.<\/span><\/p>

\u00a0<\/p>
