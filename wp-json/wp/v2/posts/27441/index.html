In the Lucidia metaverse, users have a variety of opportunities to earn $LUCID tokens, such as through the trade of non-fungible tokens (NFTs), the monetization of their art, skills, or services, the organization of events, or the leasing of virtual real estate. However, in order to enable users to earn tokens while also benefiting the network, the following four major processes have been designed:<\/span><\/p>



<\/p>

Play2Earn Lucidia has formed strategic alliances with industry-leading game development studios such as Cathoen Games, G2Esports, Playhera, and AAA Gaming Studios, amongst others, in order to utilize the Play2Earn platform to develop interactive and insightful gaming experiences. By engaging in games and getting high scores, players have the opportunity to win $LUCID tokens.<\/span><\/p>

\u00a0<\/p>

Learn2Earn<\/span><\/p>

The AI algorithms behind Lucidia’s creation of customised curriculums, which adapt to the users’ learning pace and style, have the potential to completely transform the learning experience. Users purchase courses with $LUCID tokens, and upon successful completion, they receive a return that is greater than the amount they initially spent. Lucidia hopes to entice major corporate players to migrate their training programs on the platform so they can take use of its features.<\/span><\/p>

\u00a0<\/p>

Train2Earn<\/span><\/p>

Users have the opportunity to earn tokens for their participation in the development of artificial intelligence (AI) that is housed within the metaverse. The Train2Earn mechanism provides users with an incentive to devote their time in the development of AI, which helps to cultivate a robust community of AI specialists.<\/span><\/p>

\u00a0<\/p>

Fit2Earn<\/span><\/p>

Lucidia is now offering virtual fitness classes in the metaverse. These programs are brought to you in partnership with 1rebel gym and the best personal trainers. Users are able to track the number of calories they burn using haptic devices such as the Apple Watch, and they can compete with one another on leaderboards to win incentives in the form of $LUCID tokens.
