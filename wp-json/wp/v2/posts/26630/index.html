The $LUCID token is the internal currency of the Lucidia metaverse. It is used for all transactions. Here is a detailed list of the different ways the $LUCID token can be used:<\/span><\/p>

\u00a0<\/p>

Gaming<\/span><\/p>

The $LUCID token is used to buy game items, virtual assets, NFT trading, virtual real estate, and do other in-game transactions within the Lucidia ecosystem. This makes it easier for people to play games without having to switch between currencies or payment methods. Users can also use $LUCID tokens to buy access to the game for life or to improve their avatars and profiles. Fans of the Embryo game need $LUCID tokens to feed or change their embryos, making sure that their creatures are unique and strong. $LUCID tokens also let you join tournaments with bigger prize pools.<\/span><\/p>

\u00a0<\/p>

Staking<\/span><\/p>

The staking program in Lucidia gives users rewards for keeping $LUCID tokens in a locked wallet for a certain amount of time. Rewards are given out in the form of $LUCID tokens, so users can buy more tokens and benefit from the growth of the ecosystem. Staking helps keep the network safe and gives users a way to support the platform while making passive income. To get people to stake, they will be offered things like private chats with the development team, access to VIP areas on the map, being put on a whitelist for Lucidia NFT presales, and specific monthly NFTs in exchange for voting rights in the Lucidia DAO system.<\/span><\/p>

\u00a0<\/p>

Marketplace<\/span><\/p>

The idea of the metaverse is deeply woven into Lucidia’s market. All transactions in the Lucidia marketplace will use the $LUCID token to make this integration possible.
