Lucidia Metaverse, a promising candidate for the best crypto presale of 2023, wants to add AI to its metaverse in a big way. Lucidia has come up with a new way to train AI algorithms that involves users in the process, keeps costs low, and makes sure the AI stays responsive and up-to-date. This is a solution to the common problem of training AI algorithms.<\/span><\/p>

The Train2Earn Mechanism of Lucidia:<\/span><\/p>

Train2Earn is a feature of Lucidia Metaverse that lets users train and improve the platform’s AI algorithms while getting paid for their work. This method has a number of advantages:<\/span><\/p>

Cost savings: By having users train AI, Lucidia doesn’t have to hire as many AI engineers, which lowers the cost of development.<\/span><\/p>

User-centered AI: Since the users train the AI themselves, the algorithms will be better at meeting the users’ needs and preferences.<\/span><\/p>

Up-to-date Algorithms: As users keep training the AI, the algorithms will stay up-to-date and get better over time, especially if the number of users keeps growing.<\/span><\/p>

Encouragement for Users to Join: The Train2Earn mechanism gives users another reason to join the Lucidia metaverse, which helps the platform grow.<\/span><\/p>

Availability of Platform:<\/span><\/p>

Lucidia wants to make its platform available on many devices, like Android, iOS, and PC (Windows). The first release will be a PC version that doesn’t use blockchain, but the first mobile version will work with blockchain from the start.<\/span><\/p>

Choose a Blockchain:<\/span><\/p>

Lucidia has not yet decided on a blockchain that will work well. But the main goal is to choose a gaming-focused environment with low fees and fast transactions to give users the best possible experience.<\/span><\/p>
