\u00a0<\/p>

Transaction fees are the main source of income:<\/span><\/p>

NFT Marketplace: At the heart of Lucidia Metaverse is its marketplace where NFTs can be traded for virtual goods. The platform charges a 3% transaction fee, which is about average for NFT marketplaces that are already up and running.<\/span><\/p>

\u00a0<\/p>

Royalty Fees: In addition to transaction fees, Lucidia also charges a 3% royalty fee for NFTs. This way, the creators of an NFT that is sold on the marketplace can get a cut of the sale price.<\/span><\/p>

\u00a0<\/p>

Scalability: Lucidia wants to make sure it has a steady source of income from transaction fees by getting a lot of users and helping the economy grow. By staying open to these possibilities, Lucidia can continue to grow and change to meet the changing needs of its users and the metaverse ecosystem as a whole.<\/span><\/p>
